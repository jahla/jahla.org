This is an incomplete (but in progress) list of all the anime I've seen. I will be continuing to update it and make it more accurate as time goes on.

Do I have a favourite? Yes: Black Lagoon (1st season, 2nd Barrage and Roberta's Blood Trail). It's an action show about modern day pirates in the South China Sea, with some kick-ass characters if you stick with it — see [myanimelist](http://myanimelist.net/anime/889/Black_Lagoon). I seriously recommend checking it out. 

Other notable mentions go to Desert Punk (the dub that captures the humour, not the inaccurately translated sub), Welcome to the NHK, Spice & Wolf, and Attack on Titan... could name many more but I think that's enough for now.

### Fun fact

Wanna know how I got my list of titles in alphabetical order? Using this simple little command line trick I found on [gaortzig.wordpress.com](https://gaortizg.wordpress.com/2012/01/31/how-to-sort-text-alphabetically-in-linux-using-the-command-line/):

{% highlight bash %}
cat unordered_file.txt | sort ordered file.txt
{% endhighlight %}
